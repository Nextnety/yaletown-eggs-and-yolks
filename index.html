No Statins. No Jabs. All Real Eggs.

We are Yaletowns first free range organic egg farm.

We believe in using the best in modern vertical organic chicken farming technoligies to produce the best eggs!

All of our organic eggs come from certified organic cage-free hens that have access to outdoor patios, where they are free to roam around and explore in a modern highrise environment. The yolk color is a bright orange due to the certified organic ingredients available in the chickens feed. non GMO.

We love what we do and we've been selling farm fresh eggs at our farm off Mainland St in Yaletown for more than a month.
